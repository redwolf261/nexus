# Operational Validation Report
**Phase 7.3 Intelligence Quality Audit**

## 1. Scenario: Organized Gang Robbery
- **Starting Evidence:** Analyst attaches 3 FIRs involving violent robberies along the interstate highway.
- **Analyst Actions:** Opens Workspace -> Clicks "Intelligence" tab.
- **Intelligence Generated:** 
  - *Spatial Analytics:* Corridors generated indicating a West-to-East movement pattern along NH-44.
  - *Graph Analytics:* 2 suspects extracted from the FIRs have high Betweenness Centrality in the wider district graph, linking them to an established local fence (stolen goods buyer).
- **Explainability Output:** "Suspect A linked to Suspect B via 3 historical co-arrests (Confidence 92%)."
- **Investigator Conclusion:** The system successfully guided the investigator from 3 isolated highway robberies to identifying the likely fence and the gang's movement corridor. **Validation: PASS.**

## 2. Scenario: Missing Person
- **Starting Evidence:** Analyst attaches a Missing Person report.
- **Analyst Actions:** Awaits Entity Resolution and Cross-case Overlaps.
- **Intelligence Generated:**
  - *Entity Resolution:* The engine matches the missing person's name and physical description to an Unidentified Body (UIDB) FIR registered 40km away, two days later.
- **Explainability Output:** Jaro-Winkler match (0.85) + Temporal overlap (Missing on 12th, Found on 14th) + Spatial proximity (40km). Confidence: 78%.
- **Investigator Conclusion:** The investigator cross-references the dental records. The system dramatically reduced the search space. **Validation: PASS.**

## 3. Scenario: Serial Burglar
- **Starting Evidence:** 15 residential burglaries reported over 2 months.
- **Analyst Actions:** Analyst runs Crime Series Detection on the district.
- **Intelligence Generated:**
  - *Crime Series:* DBSCAN clusters 6 of the 15 burglaries into `Series-A`.
- **Explainability Output:** "Cluster formed due to shared features: 02:00-04:00 AM (Hour), Entry: Window (MO), Target: Ground Floor (MO)."
- **Investigator Conclusion:** The analyst ignores the 9 unrelated burglaries and focuses night patrols in the hotspot generated by `Series-A`. **Validation: PASS.**

## 4. Scenario: Interstate Offender (Failure Mode)
- **Starting Evidence:** An offender commits fraud in Delhi, then in Mumbai.
- **Analyst Actions:** Analyst runs Entity Resolution.
- **Intelligence Generated:** *None.*
- **Explainability Output:** The Spatial penalty (1400km apart) outweighed the Name match. Confidence dropped below the 0.40 threshold. The engine actively suppressed the match.
- **Investigator Conclusion:** The investigator manually suspects they are the same person but the system disagrees. **Validation: FAIL (False Negative due to rigid geospatial constraints).**
